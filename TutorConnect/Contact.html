Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a tutor?

You can start by clicking the "Become a Tutor" button on our homepage or About Us page. You'll be guided through a simple registration process where you can create your profile and list your subjects of expertise.

What are the fees for students?

TutorConnect is free to join for students. You only pay for the tutoring sessions you book. Each tutor sets their own hourly rate, which is clearly displayed on their profile, allowing you to choose one that fits your budget.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, go to the Sign In page and click the "Forgot Password?" link. Enter your email address, and we will send you instructions on how to reset it.
